📖 Rules Explorer

Exponent Rules

Product Rule
\( b^m \times b^n = b^{m+n} \)
Quotient Rule
\( b^m \div b^n = b^{m-n} \)
Power of a Power
\( (b^m)^n = b^{mn} \)
Power of a Product
\( (ab)^n = a^n \times b^n \)
Zero Exponent
\( b^0 = 1 \)
Negative Exponent
\( b^{-n} = \frac{1}{b^n} \)
Fractional Exponent
\( b^{m/n} = \sqrt[n]{b^m} \)

Logarithm Rules

Product Rule
\( \log_b(xy) = \log_b(x) + \log_b(y) \)
Quotient Rule
\( \log_b\!\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) = \log_b(x) - \log_b(y) \)
Power Rule
\( \log_b(x^n) = n \cdot \log_b(x) \)
Change of Base
\( \log_b(x) = \frac{\log_c(x)}{\log_c(b)} \)
Identity Rules
\( \log_b(b) = 1 \quad\text{and}\quad \log_b(1) = 0 \quad\text{and}\quad b^{\log_b(x)} = x \)
