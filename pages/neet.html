About This Programme

NEET does not test Mathematics as a standalone subject, but strong mathematical skills are essential for Physics numericals and quantitative reasoning throughout the paper. Our NEET Mathematics programme bridges this gap — ensuring no marks are lost to calculation errors or slow working.

  • Physics-first approach — every maths topic is linked directly to NEET Physics chapters
  • Unit & dimensional analysis — a common source of errors, drilled thoroughly
  • Logarithm & exponential fluency — critical for radioactivity, optics, and thermodynamics
  • Data interpretation — graphs, tables, and comparative reasoning for Biology
  • NCERT numerical bank — all NCERT Physics numericals solved with full method

Who Is This For?

Students preparing for NEET-UG who want to strengthen the mathematical foundations that underpin the Physics and Biology sections of the paper.

  • Class 11 starters — build maths fluency alongside the NEET syllabus from the start
  • Class 12 students — consolidate weak areas before the exam season
  • NEET repeaters — target marks lost in Physics due to numerical errors
  • Biology-strong students — who want to use Maths/Physics to gain competitive edge
  • State quota aspirants — targeting government MBBS seats with high cut-off requirements
