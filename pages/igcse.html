About This Programme

Our IGCSE and Cambridge A-Level programme is delivered by tutors with direct experience of Cambridge Assessment marking schemes. Every lesson is structured around past papers, examiner reports, and mark scheme analysis — not just content delivery.

  • Cambridge syllabus-aligned — every topic mapped to the official content checklist
  • Past paper mastery — last 10 years of Cambridge past papers used systematically
  • Examiner report analysis — students learn exactly what examiners look for
  • Show-your-working culture — method marks are drilled into every response
  • International school support — aligned with school-delivered lessons and mock schedules

Who Is This For?

Students enrolled in IGCSE or A-Level programmes at international schools, or those self-studying for Cambridge examinations.

  • IGCSE Core — grades C to G, fundamental problem-solving and number skills
  • IGCSE Extended — targeting A* to C with full algebra, geometry, and calculus topics
  • AS Level — Pure 1, Mechanics 1, or Statistics 1 as standalone qualification
  • A Level — Full two-year course leading to university offers worldwide
  • Re-sit students — focused exam technique improvement for May/June or Oct/Nov series
