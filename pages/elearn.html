E-LEARN with Moodle at TutorDA

At TutorDA, we utilize Moodle, the world’s most trusted open-source Learning Management System (LMS), to provide an enriched, interactive, and curriculum-aligned e-learning experience for school and college students. Our platform ensures personalized, flexible learning supported by modern digital tools and teacher mentorship.

Key Moodle Features

  • 🎓 Aligned with CBSE, Tamil Nadu State Board, and college curriculum
  • 🧠 Interactive quizzes, assignments, and auto-graded assessments
  • 📊 Real-time tracking of student progress and performance analytics
  • 📅 Timetabled modules, deadlines, and completion monitoring
  • 🧰 Seamless integration of GeoGebra, LibreTexts, and H5P
  • 💬 Discussion forums and live chat for collaborative learning
  • 📱 Mobile-ready access through Moodle app (iOS & Android)

Benefits for Students

  • ✅ Learn anytime, anywhere with 24/7 access
  • 🎥 Access to live classes and recorded video lectures
  • 🏆 Gamified progress with badges and activity completion
  • 🔒 Secure login and personalized dashboards
  • 👨‍🏫 One-to-one mentoring and feedback from instructors
