India — Engineering Entrance

JEE Main

Joint Entrance Examination Main (NTA)
India Engineering

Conducted by the National Testing Agency, JEE Main is the gateway to NITs, IIITs, and CFTIs across India. The mathematics section (30 questions) covers the full Class 11–12 NCERT syllabus with emphasis on speed and accuracy.

JEE Advanced

Joint Entrance Examination Advanced (IIT)
India — IIT

The second stage of India's premier engineering entrance, JEE Advanced is among the world's most difficult undergraduate admission tests. Two papers of three hours each test deep conceptual understanding across calculus, algebra, coordinate geometry, and trigonometry.

BITSAT

BITS Pilani Admission Test
India Engineering

The online entrance examination for BITS Pilani campuses (Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad), with 150 questions in 180 minutes. Mathematics carries 45 questions and tests the JEE Main syllabus; speed and accuracy are critical.
