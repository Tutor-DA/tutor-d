About This Programme

Our JEE Mathematics programme goes far beyond the NCERT syllabus — developing the deep conceptual clarity and multi-step problem-solving ability needed to score high in both Mains and Advanced. We combine concept sessions with timed problem sets, full-length mocks, and strategy coaching.

  • Concept depth first — no shortcuts until the foundation is rock-solid
  • JEE-pattern questions — MCQ, integer-type, matrix-match, and passage-based problems
  • Previous year analysis — every PYQ from 2010–2024 categorised and practised
  • Speed & accuracy drills — timed chapter tests to build exam-day performance
  • Rank improvement strategy — topic prioritisation based on marks-vs-effort analysis

Who Is This For?

Students in Class 11 or 12 preparing for JEE Mains and/or Advanced, including droppers targeting a better rank in their second attempt.

  • Class 11 starters — 2-year comprehensive programme from fundamentals to advanced
  • Class 12 students — 1-year intensive with board + JEE alignment
  • JEE Mains only — focused programme targeting NIT/IIIT admission
  • JEE Advanced aspirants — extended depth for IIT-level problems
  • Droppers — structured revision and gap-filling for improved rank
