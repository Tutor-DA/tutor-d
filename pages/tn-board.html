About This Programme

Our Tamil Nadu State Board programme is fully aligned with the Samacheer Kalvi curriculum published by the Tamil Nadu Textbook and Educational Services Corporation (TNTEXTBOOK). We cover every unit from Class 6 to Class 12, preparing students for public exams and higher studies.

  • Samacheer Kalvi-first — every concept taught directly from the prescribed textbook
  • Public exam preparation — model question papers, quarterly, half-yearly, and annual exam practice
  • Tamil medium support — explanations available in Tamil for students who prefer it
  • Government school focus — structured support for students without access to private coaching
  • Scholarship exam coaching — NMMS, Talent Search, and district-level olympiad preparation

Who Is This For?

Any student studying under the Tamil Nadu State Board curriculum, from Class 6 to Class 12, whether in government, government-aided, or private schools following Samacheer Kalvi.

  • Classes 6–8 — Arithmetic, basic algebra, geometry, fractions, and measurements
  • Classes 9–10 — Algebra, real numbers, trigonometry, statistics, coordinate geometry
  • Class 11 — Sets, functions, sequences, differential calculus, statistics, probability
  • Class 12 — Applications of calculus, complex numbers, probability distributions, discrete mathematics
  • First-generation learners — special support for students with no prior tutoring experience
