⚡ Quick Tricks

To find 10%: divide by 10.
To find 5%: find 10%, then halve it.
To find 15%: find 10% + 5%.
To find 25%: divide by 4.
To find 1%: divide by 100.
Reverse %: X% of Y = Y% of X. (e.g. 8% of 25 = 25% of 8 = 2)
