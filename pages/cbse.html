About This Programme

Our CBSE Mathematics programme covers every chapter in the NCERT syllabus from Class 6 through Class 12, with special focus on board exam preparation, internal assessments, and building the strong foundation needed for JEE and NEET.

  • NCERT-first approach — every concept grounded in the textbook before extensions
  • Chapter-wise assessments — regular tests aligned with CBSE question paper pattern
  • Doubt clearing — same-day doubt resolution via WhatsApp or live session
  • Board paper practice — last 10 years of CBSE board papers covered in detail
  • Personalised study plan — tailored to each student's pace and weak areas

Who Is This For?

This programme is designed for students who need structured, curriculum-aligned support throughout their school years — from building numeracy in Class 6 to mastering calculus and vectors in Class 12.

  • Classes 6–8 — Number systems, basic algebra, geometry, mensuration
  • Classes 9–10 — Real numbers, polynomials, triangles, coordinate geometry, statistics
  • Class 11 — Sets, functions, trigonometry, limits, permutations, statistics
  • Class 12 — Calculus, vectors, 3D geometry, matrices, probability
  • Board toppers — targeting 95+ in Mathematics with strategic preparation
