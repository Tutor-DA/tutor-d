Course Developer – Mathematics

The Course Developer is responsible for designing, structuring, and deploying mathematics courses on the TutorDA platform using Moodle LMS and LaTeX.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design end-to-end math courses (school & university level)
  • Create lessons, quizzes, question banks, and assessments in Moodle
  • Develop academic content using LaTeX (notes, exams, MCQs)
  • Configure Moodle quizzes, grading, and activities
  • Ensure syllabus alignment and learning outcomes

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Strong Mathematics background
  • Hands-on Moodle LMS experience (MANDATORY)
  • Good command of LaTeX (MANDATORY)
  • Ability to debug and edit LaTeX files

Mathematics Content Developer

The Mathematics Content Developer will be responsible for creating high-quality, concept-driven mathematics learning content for TutorDA. The role focuses on clarity, structure, and academic accuracy, suitable for school and university learners.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop structured mathematics lessons aligned with syllabus
  • Create worked examples, practice problems, and assessments
  • Design MCQs, short-answer, and descriptive questions
  • Ensure conceptual correctness and logical progression
  • Collaborate with Course Developers and Video Editors

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Strong foundation in Mathematics (UG/PG level)
  • Excellent conceptual understanding and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to explain mathematics clearly and systematically

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience in academic content development or teaching
  • Basic knowledge of LaTeX for mathematical typing
  • Familiarity with CBSE / university-level curriculum

Work Mode: Remote / Part-time / Freelance

Mathematics Video Editor

The Mathematics Video Editor will transform academic math content into engaging educational videos for TutorDA’s digital platforms. This role requires both technical video editing skills and an understanding of mathematical presentation.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Edit math explainer videos, tutorials, and YouTube Shorts
  • Synchronize visuals with math explanations accurately
  • Add captions, highlights, animations, and transitions
  • Maintain TutorDA branding (colors, fonts, layout)
  • Optimize videos for vertical (9:16) and standard formats

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Proficiency in CapCut / Premiere Pro / DaVinci Resolve or similar
  • Ability to handle screen-recorded math content
  • Good sense of pacing for educational videos

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience editing educational or math-related videos
  • Basic understanding of mathematical notation
  • Familiarity with OBS screen recordings

Work Mode: Remote / Freelance / Project-based

Quality Checker – Mathematics & LaTeX

The Quality Checker plays a critical role in maintaining TutorDA’s academic standards. This position ensures that all mathematics content and LaTeX files are error-free, consistent, and publication-ready.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Review mathematical correctness of content and solutions
  • Debug, edit, and optimize LaTeX source files
  • Ensure correct formatting of equations and symbols
  • Verify MCQs, answer keys, and explanations
  • Check alignment with syllabus and learning objectives

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Strong Mathematics background
  • Good command of LaTeX (MANDATORY)
  • Ability to identify conceptual, logical, and formatting errors
  • High attention to detail and academic rigor

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with exam papers or question banks
  • Familiarity with Moodle or LMS content
  • Experience in academic publishing

Work Mode: Remote / Part-time / Freelance
