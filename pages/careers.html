Course Developer – Mathematics

The Course Developer is responsible for designing, structuring, and deploying mathematics courses on the TutorDA platform using Moodle LMS and LaTeX.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design end-to-end math courses (school & university level)
  • Create lessons, quizzes, question banks, and assessments in Moodle
  • Develop academic content using LaTeX (notes, exams, MCQs)
  • Configure Moodle quizzes, grading, and activities
  • Ensure syllabus alignment and learning outcomes

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Strong Mathematics background
  • Hands-on Moodle LMS experience (MANDATORY)
  • Good command of LaTeX (MANDATORY)
  • Ability to debug and edit LaTeX files
